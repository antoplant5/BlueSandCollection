Villa Carambola

The Place for luxurious relaxation.

Villa Carambola marks the beginning of our hospitality journey. Originally named “Casa de las dos Hermanas,” Carambola now hosts leisure travelers from all over the world. Perfect for family reunions, intimate events, and gathering of friends our house has been the setting of many memorable moments that fill us with energy and enthusiasm to continue to grow and enhance our guest experience.

Located 10 minutes from Aguadilla BQN Airport villa Carambola features over half an acre of land outlined by spectacular panoramic views of our tropical aquamarine waters. Gather your family or friends to join you at our resort-worthy home. Our elegant Villa offers plenty of outdoor space, secret stairs to the ocean, six bedrooms each with their own en suite bathroom plus two half bathrooms, a spacious interior and large outdoor infinity pool that will provide you with the comfort and relaxation our town is known for.
