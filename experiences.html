SURFING

Welcome to the North Shore of the Caribbean or like some like to call it … little Hawaii !!! Puerto Rico produces some of the biggest and most consistent surf in the Caribbean, and our town is home to some of the islands most treasured surf spots! The 8km stretch of coastline from Crash Boat to Survival beach in Aguadilla counts with about a dozen world-class surf breaks. Every season thousands of surfers visit our shores in search of the ultimate ride! Want to start your surfing journey here? We will connect you with highly experienced ISA certified surf instructors. Don’t worry they will pick a fun, beginner, mellow surf spot to make your first ride safe and enjoyable. Avid surfer but don’t know where to go ? Our concierge services can assist you with planning your surf trip! Just make sure to rent a car that can handle the bumpy ride to surf heaven! Need to rent a board? Lots of local surf shops offer wide variety of boards for rent. Contact our concierge for more information on reliable board rentals near our properties. Looking for the local surf report? Check out our regional coastal observing system, Caricoos was buoys around the island for the most reliable and robust local report: www.caricoos.org *** surf classes and trip planning must be booked and coordinated prior to check-in***

PADDLE BOARDING

Stand up paddle boarding is an inclusive sport allowing just about anyone to get up and paddling on their first time out! It offers a fun way to play on the water, with the added benefit of a full-body workout. And, since you stand at full height on your board, it gives you a unique vantage point for viewing what’s down under the water and out on the horizon!

You can choose to paddle board right in front of our properties (if conditions are appropriate and safe) or book an Eco-tour with one of our preferred partners!

We can connect you with board rentals & lessons with experienced instructors and guides.

***Reservations and tour bookings must be coordinated prior to check-in***
