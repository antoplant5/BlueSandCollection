SURFING

Welcome to the North Shore of the Caribbean or like some like to call it … little Hawaii !!! Puerto Rico produces some of the biggest and most consistent surf in the Caribbean, and our town is home to some of the islands most treasured surf spots! The 8km stretch of coastline from Crash Boat to Survival beach in Aguadilla counts with about a dozen world-class surf breaks. Every season thousands of surfers visit our shores in search of the ultimate ride! Want to start your surfing journey here?

We will connect you with highly experienced ISA certified surf instructors. Don’t worry they will pick a fun, beginner, mellow surf spot to make your first ride safe and enjoyable. Avid surfer but don’t know where to go ?

Our concierge services can assist you with planning your surf trip! Just make sure to rent a car that can handle the bumpy ride to surf heaven! Need to rent a board?

Lots of local surf shops offer wide variety of boards for rent. Contact our concierge for more information on reliable board rentals near our properties. Looking for the local surf report?

Check out our regional coastal observing system, Caricoos was buoys around the island for the most reliable and robust local report: www.caricoos.org

***Surf classes and trip planning must be booked and coordinated prior to check-in***

PADDLE BOARDING

Stand up paddle boarding is an inclusive sport allowing just about anyone to get up and paddling on their first time out! It offers a fun way to play on the water, with the added benefit of a full-body workout. And, since you stand at full height on your board, it gives you a unique vantage point for viewing what’s down under the water and out on the horizon!

You can choose to paddle board right in front of our properties (if conditions are appropriate and safe) or book an Eco-tour with one of our preferred partners!

We can connect you with board rentals & lessons with experienced instructors and guides.

***Reservations and tour bookings must be coordinated prior to check-in***

SCUBA DIVING

Scuba diving in Aguadilla is a must! The astounding visibility of are clear warm waters allows divers of all levels to explore pristine coral reefs, caves, and untouched offshore islands.

Our preferred local dive shop Aquatica offers both shore and boat dives off the Northwest coast of Puerto Rico. From pristine reefs to underwater caves, our dive sites are some of the best locations on the island! Join us year-round for fantastic diving. If you’re new to diving Aquatica has the perfect beach location for you to learn and build your new skills. 

Aquatica PR offers both scuba diving tours and PADI and SSI certification courses if you wish to get certified during your trip contact our concierge for details ! Aquatica will set you up with the online course you will need prior to jumping in the ocean so once you arrive all that’s left to do is the fun part!

***Reservations and tour bookings must be coordinated prior to check-in. SCUBA certification classes must be coordinated at least 3 months prior to check-in date***

HIKING

Survival Beach Hike


10 minute drive from the house.! Survival Beach is a long stretch of sand in Aguadilla, with some really neat rock formations along the shoreline. It is only a 30 to 40-minue hike along a path through the trees and over rocks at the beach. It is not super easy, but most people should be able to make the trek to enjoy this place. You must park at "surfers beach" then venture into the woods! Locals are nice and welcoming so make sure to ask them what's the best path to follow.
How to get there? Type “Surfers Beach on Google Maps!
Parking: Park at surfers beach

Playuela Scenic Walk


Just a short walk from the house you will find the hidden valley of Playuela. A long stretch of undeveloped land filled with beautiful palm trees, native species, and a beautiful coast line. Ask our concierge for directions and take an exciting walk through the neighborhood!

Gozalandia Waterfalls


A 45 minute car drive will take you to one of the most awe inspiring views on the island: a unique 60ft waterfall flowing over a gaping cave and descending into a large and deep pool of turquoise and emerald waters. Bring your bathing suit and go for a swim in this natural beauty immersed in the mountains of our tropical forests.. For those who seek the thrill, some of the audacious locals jump from the inside of the waterfall! If you want more, to the right of the main waterfall you will find a dirt path that borders the river and takes you up to another spectacular waterfall that showers into another beautiful pool. In here, you will also find a rope attached to a tree which locals use to swing themselves into the swimming hole! An easy stair-walk from the parking gets you there in 5 minutes. If you prefer the comfort of a having a tour-guide, then contact Verde Azul for a group expedition to this wondrous place.

BEACH

Crash Boat Beach

Located just a 5 minute drive for our luxury villas and right in front of our Cocoloba casitas Crash Boat is one of the most iconic, popular, and beautiful public beaches in Puerto Rico. Known by locals as “El Crash” this beach still has remains of the piers built by the armed forces decades ago !! Now this old piers serve as a fishing spot and for jumping into the water. Known for its sandy beach and crystalline waters el Crash is the best swimming spot of town! Crash Boat also has two large sand beach areas for general bathing. The beach is primarily accessed by taking PR-107 to a spur signed, PR-458 and counts with parking.

Borinquen Beach

Located about a 5 minute drive from our luxury villas you will find Borinquen beach, one of the most scenic beaches in town and our personal favorite. Perfect for beautiful sunsets. If you are looking for a quiet beach this is the one, wide open, beautiful views, and a picnic with ocean breeze. Located just a few hundred yards from the old lighthouse ruins, this beach is a great place to have a picnic lunch under the palm trees. It is a large beach, on both sides of the parking lot … I doubt you will ever feel crowded here! The beach is access through the 107 road turning right on the gold course entrance. Parking on site.

Peña Blanca Beach

Peña Blanca is located a walks or swim distance from the house. It is a small beach and it welcomes visitors from late May until August and depending on the tide. From fall to early spring, the tide goes all the way to the cliffs and the beach disappears this is a natural phenomenon that happens on our region yearly. Lucky ones to be the first to visit the beach for the summer season, get to feel like they just discovered a new beach, each year it looks a little different with a brand new thick carpet of clean sand, it is a naturally landscaped beach so visitors will find objects washed ashore.  Over the past few years, this beach has gained popularity among Puerto Ricans and travelers searching for Puerto Rico’s special off the beaten paths.  Playa Peña Blanca is a dreamy beach, set apart by its landscape and a place you should consider snorkeling at! You will walk down some old concrete steps leading down to shaded area where you’ll stand to enjoy a panoramic view of this beautiful beach.  A narrow pathway with concrete steps leads to the beach. Not much parking space available.

Rompe Olas Beach

Located about 10-15 minute drive from our luxury villas. Rompeolas Beach offers some of the clearest water along the northwest coast of Puerto Rico, with gorgeous shades of turquoise.  Known for its rock jetty that was created to control the surf.  The beach area closest to the jetty offers the best swimming water all year round.  This beach spot is the best for small children in Aguadilla.  Keep in mind there are no lifeguards on the beach, you swim at your own risk.   Rompeolas is a popular beach among locals, but it doesn’t get as full of Crashboat.  Summer times is the busiest time with mostly local families.  During the winter months, you may be sharing the beach with a few families and vacationer on weekdays.  Parking available.
