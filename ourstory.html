Be a part of our Story!

Blue Sand Collection was founded by local business owners Alberto Cordero and Shannon Boyle, a husband and wife team with the idea of creating unique experiences in the west coast of Puerto Rico. Today BSC has grown to be one of the leaders in the short term rental industry of our town hosting travelers from all over the world and proudly employing local talent that keep innovating to bring you memorable experiences worth repeating.

Villa Carambola & Villa Flamboyan marked the beginning of BSC hospitality journey. Today we embark on the most challenging and ambitious projects yet “Cocoloba” a collection of rustic-chic beachfront “casitas” focused on adventure, connection with nature, style, and conservation. Cocoloba also features an eco-friendly beach bar serving local delicacies and hosting live music, and community events for the enjoyment of those that decide to be part of our story.

Blue Sand Collection has served as the setting of many memorable moments that fill us with energy and enthusiasm to continue to grow and enhance your guest experience. We pride ourselves on offering the outstanding hospitality that characterizes our beautiful island. Focusing our attention on the details that will make your experience unique by offering a diversity of services and helping you create moments you will never forget… We invite you to come to the place where luxury meets relaxation and adventure!

Located on a site of Historical Significance

Aguadilla is the perfect headquarters for exploring North west Puerto Rico. The natural assets surrounding our villas have been key contributors to the development of its coastal communities. The same “wind protected” characteristic that makes this region a surf paradise drove the Spanish colonizers to construct a port in what was known back the as “North Aguada” during the 17 th century. Since then, the fortunes of our coast have paved the way for the cultural and economic growth that the area has experienced until the present. From surfing, to diving and fishing, littoral residents have been able to develop businesses around hospitality, gastronomy, and recreation. We are proud to be one of the local businesses paving the way for sustainable development and economic growth in the area.

Our Team

Founders & Owners - Alberto Cordero & Shannon Boyle

Alberto Cordero & Shannon Boyle met while pursuing their degrees at Purdue University. Throughout their years together they have been able to visit many places around the world. Inspired by their travels & the beauty of Aguadilla, Alberto’s hometown, they decided to develop a company that invited others to experience the same beauty that led them to establish a life and family in Puerto Rico.

Although they lived in San Juan with their two kids, a lot of their time was spent driving out to Aguadilla to visit Alberto's family. Aguadilla became a big part of Shannon's life and eventually the Cordero’s second home.

With Shannon's and Alberto´s vision and impeccable taste, they have built and designed beautiful properties and develop an eco-conscious beach bar at Crash Boat Beach. Today Villa Carambola, Villa Flamboyan, Cocoloba, and Cocoloba Beach Bar provide unique experiences on the west coast of Puerto Rico for travelers from all over the world to enjoy!
